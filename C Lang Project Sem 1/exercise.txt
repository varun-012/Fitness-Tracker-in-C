Along with Proper Diet we also need to perform Physical Activity in our Fitness Journey

There are many types of physical activities that you can perform :

1) Strength Training / Resistance Training
2) Mixed Martial Arts
3) Walking / Jogging
4) Hiking
5) Bicycling
6) Swimming 
7) Hatha Yoga
8) Aerobics
9) Dancing
10) Weight Lifting and many more...
You can also play your favourite Sport of your choice 

Note : All the activities initially should be done in the supervision of a Qualified Trainer.
